The Indicadores is designed by UNIFESP to facilitate the dissemination of university data and indicators using Business Intelligence. It uses [_Pentaho_](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pentaho) and [_Oncase Oktopus_](https://github.com/oncase/oktopus-pentaho) as a frontend.

**My role in this project** was to develop a comprehensive visual identity, including the creation of a logo mark, visual identity, **frontend redesign**, a online manual of [Forplad indicators](http://www.forplad.andifes.org.br/?q=documentosdiversos), and a few pages more pages: _About_, _Help_, _Login_, _404 Page_, etc

## Rebranding

Indicators were born as a project without a logo or visual identity. And the _Oncase Oktopus_ interface doesn't offer any visual appeal.

## Interface

The frontend of Indicadores was implemented using a frontend called [Oktopus](https://github.com/oncase/oktopus-pentaho) developed by Oncase. It was not the intention to do strong changes in interface, just fine adjustments and a new palette color. At this stage, the bulk of my work consisted of mapping and rewriting CSS classes to get a nicer look.
