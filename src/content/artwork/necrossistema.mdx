"An initiative by the Center for Society, University, and Science Studies (SoU_Ciência) at the Federal University of São Paulo (Unifesp) with the goal of describing and interpreting how agents and institutions acted during the COVID-19 pandemic against scientific evidence and medical ethics, hindering the effective management of public policy and compromising the assurance of the right to life for our citizens.

This initiative has produced the collection 'Evidence of Necropolitics during the Pandemic' and the 'Necrosystem in the COVID-19 Pandemic' map."

**My role in this project** was concept and create all graphic communication of this project, since visual identity to website prototype, including all necessary illustrations to the map and the website. Know more about the [tech side of this project here](/work/necrossistema).
